Ahmedabad

The cultural and economic heart of Gujarat, known for its rich history, vibrant festivals, and delicious cuisine.

Top Attractions

  • Sabarmati Ashram - The historic home of Mahatma Gandhi, a place of peace and reflection.
  • Kankaria Lake - A picturesque lake with a zoo, toy train, and balloon rides.
  • Adalaj Stepwell - A stunning example of Indo-Islamic architecture and craftsmanship.
  • Swaminarayan Temple - A beautifully carved temple dedicated to Lord Swaminarayan.
  • Sidi Saiyyed Mosque - Famous for its intricate stone latticework windows.

Travel Tips

Best time to visit: November to February, when the weather is pleasant.

Must-try food: Dhokla, Khandvi, and the famous Gujarati Thali.
