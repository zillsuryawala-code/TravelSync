Rajkot

The vibrant city of Rajkot blends history, culture, and modernity, offering visitors a taste of traditional Saurashtra with a modern twist.

Top Attractions

  • Jubilee Garden - A beautiful park surrounded by historical monuments and a favorite spot for locals.
  • Rotary Dolls Museum - A unique museum showcasing dolls from around the world, celebrating global cultures.
  • Watson Museum - Dedicated to colonial history and local heritage with a vast collection of artifacts.
  • Race Course Grounds - A large recreational area popular for evening walks, sports, and festivals.
  • Khambhalida Caves - Ancient Buddhist caves carved into limestone rock, offering a glimpse into the past.

Travel Tips

Best time to visit: October to March, when the temperatures are cooler and ideal for sightseeing.

Must-try food: Kathiyawadi Thali, Fafda-Jalebi, and Rajkot's famous Ganthiya.
