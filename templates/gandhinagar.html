Gandhinagar

The serene and well-planned capital city of Gujarat, known for its greenery and architectural marvels.

Top Attractions

  • Akshardham Temple - A grand temple dedicated to Lord Swaminarayan, known for its stunning architecture.
  • Children's Park - A fun park with boating facilities, a mini-train, and lush gardens.
  • Indroda Nature Park - Known as India's Jurassic Park, it houses a zoo, botanical garden, and dinosaur fossils.
  • Sarita Udyan - A popular picnic spot along the banks of the Sabarmati River.

Travel Tips

Best time to visit: November to February for pleasant weather.

Must-try food: Gujarati Thali, Dhokla, and Handvo.
