Vadodara (Baroda)

The cultural capital of Gujarat, known for its palaces, museums, and lush gardens.

Top Attractions

  • Laxmi Vilas Palace - A stunning royal residence with beautiful architecture and a museum.
  • Sayaji Baug - A sprawling garden with a zoo, planetarium, and toy train.
  • Baroda Museum - A museum showcasing art, sculpture, and historical artifacts.
  • Sursagar Lake - A serene lake with a towering statue of Lord Shiva in the middle.

Travel Tips

Best time to visit: November to February, when the weather is cool and pleasant.

Must-try food: Sev Usal, Khaman, and the famous Gujarati Thali.

Local festivals: Don't miss the Navratri celebrations in Vadodara!
