Frequently Asked Questions

How do I make changes to my booking?

You can modify your booking by logging into your account and accessing the "My Bookings" section. For major changes or assistance, please contact our customer support team.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellation policies vary by hotel and rate plan. You can find the specific cancellation policy for your booking in the confirmation email or by checking the hotel details. Generally, cancellations made 48 hours before check-in receive a full refund.

How can I contact customer support?

Our customer support team is available 24/7 via phone at +91 98765 43210 or through the live chat feature on our website. You can also email us at support@travelsync.com.

Do you offer corporate or group discounts?

Yes, we offer special rates for corporate clients and group bookings. Please contact our business travel department at corporate@travelsync.com for more information.
