Our Story

Discover the journey behind TravelSync and our passion for creating unforgettable travel experiences

Who We Are

Founded in 2020, TravelSync began as a small team of travel enthusiasts with a big dream: to make travel planning seamless and enjoyable for everyone. What started as a local travel agency in Ahmedabad has now grown into one of Gujarat's leading online travel platforms.

Our mission is simple - to connect travelers with the best accommodations and experiences while providing exceptional service at every step of the journey. We believe that travel should be about creating memories, not stress.

Today, we're proud to serve thousands of happy customers across India, with a special focus on showcasing the incredible hospitality of Gujarat.

Our Journey

From our humble beginnings with just three employees and a single office, we've grown exponentially while maintaining our commitment to personalized service. Our growth has been fueled by our dedication to innovation and customer satisfaction.

In 2021, we launched our online platform, revolutionizing how travelers in Gujarat book accommodations. By 2023, we had partnered with over 500 hotels across the state, and in 2024, we expanded our services to include curated travel experiences.

Despite our growth, we've remained true to our roots, with our headquarters still in Ahmedabad and most of our team hailing from Gujarat communities.

Our Mission

To simplify travel by connecting people with the perfect accommodations while delivering exceptional service that exceeds expectations at every touchpoint.

Our Vision

To become Gujarat's most trusted travel platform, known for our innovative solutions, local expertise, and commitment to creating memorable experiences.
