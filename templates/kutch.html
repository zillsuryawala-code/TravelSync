Rann of Kutch

Famous for the mesmerizing White Desert (Rann of Kutch) and vibrant handicrafts, culture, and festivals.

Top Attractions

  • Great Rann of Kutch - A stunning salt desert that glows under the full moon.
  • Kala Dungar - The highest point of Kutch offering panoramic views of the Rann.
  • Kutch Museum - Gujarat’s oldest museum showcasing tribal culture.
  • Mandvi Beach - A scenic beach with a historic palace nearby.

Travel Tips

Best time to visit: November to February during Rann Utsav festival.

Must-try food: Kutchi Dabeli and Bajra no Rotlo.
