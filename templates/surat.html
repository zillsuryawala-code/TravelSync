Surat

The diamond city of India, known for its thriving textile industry, delicious street food, and vibrant culture.

Top Attractions

  • Sarthana Nature Park - A lush green park with a zoo and a variety of flora and fauna.
  • Sardar Patel Museum - A museum showcasing artifacts, sculptures, and historical exhibits.
  • Dutch Garden - A serene garden with Dutch-style architecture and beautiful fountains.
  • Gopi Talav - A historic lake surrounded by gardens and walking paths.

Travel Tips

Best time to visit: October to March, when the weather is pleasant.

Must-try food: Locho, Ghari, and the famous Surati street food.

Local festivals: Don't miss the Navratri celebrations in Surat!
