Dwarka

One of the sacred Char Dham pilgrimage sites, believed to be the ancient kingdom of Lord Krishna.

Top Attractions

  • Dwarkadhish Temple - A 2000-year-old temple dedicated to Lord Krishna.
  • Nageshwar Jyotirlinga - One of the 12 revered Jyotirlinga shrines of Shiva.
  • Bet Dwarka - An island believed to be the original residence of Lord Krishna.
  • Rukmini Devi Temple - Dedicated to Lord Krishna's chief queen, Rukmini.

Travel Tips

Best time to visit: October to March for festivals and calm weather.

Must-try food: Local Gujarati snacks, Khichdi, and Farsan.
