Somnath

A spiritual and historic destination, home to the first Jyotirlinga of Lord Shiva on the Arabian Sea coast.

Top Attractions

  • Somnath Temple - A magnificent temple symbolizing resilience through centuries.
  • Bhalka Tirth - The place where Lord Krishna was mistakenly shot by a hunter.
  • Triveni Sangam - The confluence of three rivers, considered sacred.
  • Panch Pandav Gufa - A historic cave associated with the Pandavas from Mahabharata.

Travel Tips

Best time to visit: November to February for cooler and pleasant climate.

Must-try food: Sweets like Peda and traditional Gujarati cuisine.
