Bhuj

Gateway to the Rann of Kutch, Bhuj is rich with historical sites, palaces, and traditional crafts.

Top Attractions

  • Aina Mahal - A beautiful palace with intricate mirror work.
  • Prag Mahal - A stunning 19th-century palace with a European touch.
  • Hamirsar Lake - A peaceful spot perfect for relaxation and picnics.
  • Bhujodi - A handicraft village showcasing traditional weaving and crafts.

Travel Tips

Best time to visit: October to March for cool weather and fairs.

Must-try food: Bhakri, Khichdi, and Kutchi sweets like Gulab Pak.
